User manual

-

1. You can press the date on the calendar and change it to the date you want.

2. You can drag the line plots to zoom in and double click to reset

3. You can scroll the mouse wheel on the map to zoom in and out

4. The red dotted line in the bottom left plot represents recommended sleep time(5~9hours)

5. The red dotted line in the bottom right plot represents our threshold which is used for calculating depression level

6. You can change the month and date using each arrows on the top
